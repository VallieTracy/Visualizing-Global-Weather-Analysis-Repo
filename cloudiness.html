Cloudiness

Doesn't initially appear to be a strong correlation between cloudiness and latitude. It's interesting to see clusters of similar cloudiness along a spectrum of latitude. Could be worth exploring further. Similar to humidity v latitude, our r-values are low for both hemispheres when charting cloudiness v latitude. Again, my guess is we could correlate cloudiness with other markers, just not latitude (alone).
