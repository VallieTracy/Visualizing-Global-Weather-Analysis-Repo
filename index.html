Summary: Latitude vs. X

For this project, I wanted to answer the question of how weather varies in relation to distance from the equator. To accomplish this, I analyzed 500+ cities from across the globe to build a representative global weather model. I utilized a simple Python library & the OpenWeatherMap API.

I focused on how latitude relates to: Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, & Wind Speed. I ulitmately found that there does appear to be a strong correlation between max temperature and latitude. There doesn't appear to be a strong correlation between latitude and humidity, cloudiness, or wind speed. And max temp looks to decrease as we move away from the equator, both north and south.
