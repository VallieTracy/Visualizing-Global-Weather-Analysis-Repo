Humidity

Based off of the scatter plot alone, there does not appear to be any correlation between humidity and latitude. I also performed a linear regression (not seen here), which resulted in low r-values across both hemispheres, indicating weak correlation. Perhaps there would be a stronger correlation if we narrowed down latitude and added in longitutde. But across the entirety of the globe, our r-value indicates there is not a strong correlation.
